# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 19, 2024

## Introduction

Entretien AI ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI interview practice platform and website (collectively, the "Service").

## Information We Collect

### Authentication Information
When you sign in using GitHub or Gmail, we collect only:
- Your email address
- Your username
- Your profile picture

### Interview Practice Data
We collect and process:
- Resume data you upload for interview preparation
- Voice recordings during practice sessions
- Interview responses and transcripts
- Performance metrics and scores
- Session duration and timestamps

### Technical Information
We automatically collect:
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- IP address
- Session duration
- Pages visited

## How We Use Your Information

### Core Service Functionality
1. Generating personalized interview questions
2. Providing AI-powered feedback
3. Tracking your interview practice progress
4. Maintaining your user account

### Service Improvement
1. Analyzing usage patterns to enhance our AI models
2. Identifying and fixing technical issues
3. Developing new features
4. Improving question generation accuracy

## Data Storage and Security

### Storage Duration
- Authentication data: Stored while your account is active
- Interview recordings: Automatically deleted after 30 days
- Interview transcripts: Stored for 90 days
- Performance metrics: Retained for 12 months

### Security Measures
- End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
- Regular security audits and penetration testing
- Secure cloud infrastructure with AWS/Azure
- Regular backup procedures
- Access control and authentication protocols

## Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

### We Share Data With
- Authentication providers (GitHub, Gmail)
- Cloud infrastructure providers
- Analytics services (anonymized data only)

### We Never Share
- Your resume content
- Interview recordings
- Personal feedback data
- Individual performance metrics

## Your Data Rights

You have the right to:
1. Access your personal data
2. Request data deletion
4. Modify your profile information
5. Opt-out of non-essential data collection
6. Request data correction

## Cookie Policy

We use cookies for:
### Essential Cookies
- Authentication status
- Session management
- Security features

### Optional Cookies
- Analytics
- Performance monitoring
- Feature preferences

## Children's Privacy

- Service is not intended for users under 13
- We do not knowingly collect data from children
- Parents can request data deletion if collected inadvertently

## Interview Data Processing

### Collection

- Responses are analyzed using AI models
- Performance metrics are calculated automatically

### Storage
- Recordings in audio format are not stored
- Data is stored in secure cloud infrastructure
- Regular data purging based on retention policy

### Usage
- Performance analysis and feedback
- Service improvement
